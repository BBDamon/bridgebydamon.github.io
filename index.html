Beginners course

This 12-week course is for people completely new to bridge, looking to learn the game. In this course, you will learn how to play, the basics of the Standard American bidding system, and some important card play skills.

FAQ:

1) Is there a trial period? Yep, free to drop after 4 weeks if it's not your cup of tea.
2) I can't attend all the sessions - can I still attend? If you can make at least 8 of the 12 sessions, you're most welcome to attend. I will support you so that you can slot back in smoothly after a missed session.
3) Do I need to come with a partner? Ideally yes. However if you come as a single, I can slot you in with other singles floating around.
4) Do I need to pay a deposit? No. Just pay on the day (or shortly after the session).
5) I can't make daytime classes - will you be running an evening course? Register and mention that you're an evening-only person and I will notify you when the next evening course is available.
