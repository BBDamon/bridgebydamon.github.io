FAQ:

1) I've come from a different beginners course - can I join yours? Yes. Just give me a call on 0408 862 065 and we can chat about what you've learned so far and if this course is the right next step.
2) I can't attend all the sessions - can I still attend? If you can make at least 5 of the 8 sessions, you're most welcome to attend. I will support you so that you can slot back in smoothly after a missed session.
3) Do I need to come with a partner? Ideally yes. However if you come as a single, I can slot you in with other singles floating around.
4) Do I need to pay a deposit? No. Just pay on the day (or shortly after the session).
